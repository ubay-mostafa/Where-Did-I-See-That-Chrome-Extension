Privacy Policy

Extension: Where Did I See That?

Last Updated: March 22, 2026

TL;DR: All your data stays on your device. We don't send anything to external servers, ever.

Overview

Your privacy is our top priority. This extension stores all data locally on your device using your browser's IndexedDB storage. No information is ever transmitted to external servers, third parties, or the extension developers.

What Data We Collect

The extension collects and stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Web History: URLs of pages you visit
  • Page Titles: The title of each webpage
  • Website Content: Text content from webpages (up to 50,000 characters per page)
  • Visit Timestamps: When you visited each page
  • Domain Names: The website domain (e.g., github.com)
  • Visit Count: How many times you've visited each page

What We DON'T Collect

The extension explicitly does NOT collect, store, or access:

  • ❌ Passwords or login credentials
  • ❌ Payment or financial information
  • ❌ Form data or input fields
  • ❌ Personal communications (emails, messages)
  • ❌ Authentication tokens or cookies
  • ❌ Location data or IP addresses
  • ❌ User activity (keystrokes, mouse movements)
  • ❌ Private/incognito browsing data

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for the extension's core functionality:

  • Enabling keyword search through your browsing history
  • Displaying search results with relevant page snippets
  • Ranking results by relevance, recency, and visit frequency

That's it. No analytics, no tracking, no other purposes.

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage Only: All data is stored in your browser's IndexedDB database on your device
  • No Cloud Sync: Data never leaves your computer
  • No External Servers: The extension does not connect to any external servers
  • Automatic Cleanup: Data older than 30 days is automatically deleted
  • Storage Limit: Content is limited to 50,000 characters per page

Privacy Protection Features

The extension includes built-in privacy protections:

  • Automatic Blacklist: Banking sites, login pages, and sensitive domains are never tracked
  • Sites with "banking", "login", "signin", "auth" in the URL are excluded
  • localhost and development servers (127.0.0.1) are excluded
  • Google account pages and similar sensitive services are excluded
  • Incognito/private browsing is never tracked

Third-Party Data Sharing

We do NOT:

  • ❌ Share your data with third parties
  • ❌ Sell your data to anyone
  • ❌ Transmit your data over the internet
  • ❌ Use analytics or tracking services
  • ❌ Collect telemetry or usage statistics

Data Retention

  • Data is automatically deleted after 30 days
  • You can manually clear all data at any time by uninstalling the extension
  • You can clear browser data through Chrome's settings (chrome://settings/clearBrowserData)

Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is in your browser's local storage
  • Delete: Uninstall the extension to remove all data
  • Export: Data can be accessed via browser developer tools
  • Exclude Sites: Sensitive domains are automatically excluded

Children's Privacy

This extension does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. All data collection is local and automatic based on browsing behavior.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in the extension's functionality. The "Last Updated" date at the top will be revised accordingly. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Open Source and Transparency

This extension's code is transparent and auditable. All data handling is done locally in your browser with no external connections.

Technical Details for Developers

For those interested in the technical implementation:

  • Storage: IndexedDB (browser's local database)
  • Database Name: TabMemoryDB
  • Maximum Age: 30 days
  • Content Limit: 50,000 characters per page
  • No external API calls or network requests
  • All processing done client-side in the browser
Summary: Your browsing history stays on your device. We can't see it, we don't want to see it, and we've built the extension to make sure nobody else can either.
